Setting up application:
1. Under the database folder, go to load.sql and copy the contents of the file. 
2. On your Google Chrome browser, head to your local PHPMyAdmin database ("http://localhost/phpmyadmin/"). 
3. Select the SQL tab, paste the copied contents in the input field and press Go. 
4. Under "registration.php" AND "validation.php", change your username and password accordingly if needed. 
   Do ensure that the fields are the same for all the files. 
   (by default, username: root, password: , mac may have it as root though)
